technology.

Built to be efficiently broken down and recycled, the new Circaloft styles meet our Circular Design requirements. Crafted with synthetic insulation, the versatile construction allows for enhanced durability while still maintaining a soft hand feel, and helps retain warmth when wet.

Keep the good going.

We crafted Circaloft for the future, applying our Circular Design principles to help keep it in use and out of landfills. It’s built to last and designed to be made into something new at the end of its usable life.
